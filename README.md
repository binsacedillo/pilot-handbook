> **A guided flight training companion designed to help student pilots understand, calculate, and verify critical preflight decisions with clarity and confidence.**

---

## 🧭 Purpose

The Pilot Handbook is more than just a calculator; it is a step-by-step mentor for your training journey. It addresses the core anxieties of learning to fly:
- **"Am I doing this correctly?"** — Guided workflows ensure you follow the right steps every time.
- **"What does the POH actually mean?"** — Our engine translates raw numbers into plain-language safety implications.
- **"Is this safe?"** — Instant visual feedback on weight, fuel, and performance margins.

### The Student Pilot Assistant Experience
- **Guided Decision Checks**: Step-by-step assistant for Aircraft Loading, Fuel Planning, and Performance Analysis.
- **Explanation Layer**: Every result includes a short explanation of the safety implications and a "tip for next time."
- **Professional Habits**: Refine your preflight routine to match industry standards from day one.
- **Verifiable Records**: Maintain structured, instructor-verifiable flight logs.

---

## 🗺️ Development Roadmap

### ✅ Core Capabilities
- **Student-First UI**: A clear, reliable environment designed for fast, consistent preflight planning.
- **Guided Preflight Workflow**: A modular assistant that walks you through Loading, Performance, and Fuel safety checks.
- **Decision Mentorship**: Integrated logic that reinforces safe decision-making habits and explains the *why* behind the math.
- **Cloud Logbook**: Secure, permanent storage for pilot records and aircraft performance snapshots.

### 🚧 Future Features
- **Learning Feedback Loop**: Insight into past calculations to highlight improvements and common mistakes.
- **Instructor Portal**: Shared flight snapshots for preflight signatures and verification.
- **Glossary & Resource Integration**: "Learn More" links connecting calculations directly to FAA handbook concepts.

## 🏗️ Technical Foundation

The Pilot Handbook is built on a modern, reliable stack (Next.js 16, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS v4, tRPC, Prisma) and follows a **Mentor-First** architectural philosophy.
